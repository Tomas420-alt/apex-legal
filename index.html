Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

KyroTune collects the following information when you use our app:

  • Account information (name, email) via Apple Sign-In
  • Motorcycle details you provide (make, model, year, mileage)
  • Maintenance records, inspection data, and service history
  • Subscription status
  • Device push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate personalised AI maintenance plans
  • Track your service history and upcoming tasks
  • Send maintenance reminders (if enabled)
  • Process your subscription

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and stored securely on Convex cloud infrastructure. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties except as required to operate the service.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Convex (database & backend)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • OpenAI (AI maintenance plan generation — de-identified bike specifications only)
  • Sentry (error tracking — crash reports and stack traces in production only, no personal data)

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
